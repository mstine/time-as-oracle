“
An oracle cannot predict a precise event. But it can cast an image of a certain qualitative field of events — a certain psychological probability.
— Marie-Louise von Franz · On Divination and Synchronicity
THE HINGE — say it slowly. Synchronicity (Jung's acausal connecting principle, sharpened by his closest collaborator) is MEANINGFUL coincidence, not frequent coincidence — and von Franz named the mechanism sixty years before I ran a line of SQL: divination never claimed to name the precise card. It conditions a qualitative FIELD. So the skeptic and I have been arguing two different questions without noticing. Did a specific card beat chance? — that's frequency. Does the field, the charge, the element track what I'm living? — that's meaning. Two questions; they do NOT have the same answer. The next slide gives both. And it's why the Five of Pentacles mattered and a Three of Cups in that same autumn would have meant nothing: synchronicity is the MATCH between symbol and season, never the count.
So I asked von Franz's two questions
Not the card. The field.
The precise card? Chance. No card beats the odds — even the Five of Pentacles that haunted me: my deck clusters some card like that a third of the time.

The qualitative field? Real. The element keeps time with the moment — what I ask and what I live, both. Earth · money   Fire · making   Water · loss   Air + Water · the Morrigan.

The deck didn't pick the card. It kept time with the charge.
This is the reveal — von Franz, measured. TWO questions, two answers; do NOT let the room hear 'null = the cards are fake.' (1) THE PRECISE EVENT (frequency): null. Permutation + multiple-comparison correction; nothing survives; the card table sits below chance. Callback to the hook: the Five of Pentacles that opened the talk was itself chance at the count (p=0.37) — say it, I tested my own anecdote and the NUMBER was noise. (2) THE QUALITATIVE FIELD (meaning): real. The element/suit tracks the moment two independent ways (what I ask; what I'm living), each p=0.042, calendar held fixed — that IS von Franz's 'qualitative field of events,' empirically. The synchronicity was never how OFTEN the Five of Pentacles came; it was that of 78 cards the scarcity card kept time with the scarcity season — and the element finding is that same correspondence at the grain coarse enough to measure. Earth=money, Fire=making, Water=love/loss; the Morrigan reads Air+Water (war-crow + washer at the ford), found exactly where I already knew her. Discipline: marginal (just under 0.05), single-author, replicate-to-lock; the omnibus certifies the whole map, never a single pair. HOLD BOTH registers — the frequency is chance AND the field is meaningful; never use one to dodge the other. OPTIONAL SPOKEN ASIDE (NOT on the slide — drop it live only if the room is with you; cut without consequence if not): both element tests landed at the same p-value, 0.042 — and 42 has been my number since The Hitchhiker's Guide as a kid, the Answer to Life, the Universe, and Everything. I know exactly what that p is: where the shuffles happened to land, twice; it means nothing to the math that it spells my favorite number. I'm telling you anyway, because that gap — pure chance to the data, a private wink to me, both held at once — IS the whole talk. The permutation doesn't care it came up 42. I do.
